CCCC MUSIC LAB

Art Direction + Graphic Design — 2022

CCCC stands for Centre del Carme Cultura Contemporània, a museum located in the heart of Valencia. The project started trough the rehabilitaion of an old church wich now hosts art of all kind. Even thought it started as we know it in 2017, it wasn't until 2019 it's music department, CCCC Music Lab, started.

The concept is simple and it is to create a public and horizontal label for managing, publicing and enhancing music projects made by independent young artists. It started as a Doctor's Degree and has became a succesfull reality. In 2022, CCCC Music Lab is becoming independent as a graphic entitiy from Centre del Carme Cultura Contemporània and I've been commisioned to make it a reality.
